Computational Design Workflows

Spatial
Canvases

This gallery presents seven computational canvases made with p5.js, Three.js, D3.js, and Mapbox. The works explore fractals, underwater environments, glowing geometries, symbolic organisms, temporal data, flowering cycles, relational systems, authorship, and spatial atmosphere.
